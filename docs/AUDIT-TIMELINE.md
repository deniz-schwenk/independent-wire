## How to navigate the trail

If you are **debugging an unexpected Curator output**, start with the recalibrated audit row's per-topic reports — they document the on/off rate for every audited top-10 topic at the production constants, with reasoning notes per finding. From there, drop into the corresponding finding-text bundle under `_data/{date}/topic-NN.json`.

If you are **reasoning about the threshold**, the recalibration sweep's `sweep.md` carries the full 12-config table (off %, recall, orphan %, max gravity-trap off, min trap retained) plus per-(audited topic) detail across all 12 configurations and the dual-constraint pass that filtered the architect's choice. The qualitative samples under `samples/` carry the per-finding view across the three top configurations for the gravity-trap / borderline / clean reference topics.

If you are **planning a future calibration**, the brief-level rationale lives in `docs/ADR-CURATOR-TRIPLE-STAGE.md` (the architectural decision) and the brief documents themselves (`TASK-EMBED-PRE-CLUSTER-STAGE.md`, `TASK-GRAVITATIONAL-ASSIGN-STAGE.md`, `TASK-CURATOR-TOPIC-DISCOVERY-STAGE.md`, `TASK-TRIPLE-STAGE-CUTOVER.md`, `TASK-CLUSTER-QUALITY-AUDIT.md`, `TASK-GRAVITATIONAL-RECALIBRATION.md` — all gitignored as architect-local). The pinned-constant docstrings in `src/stages/pre_cluster.py`, `src/stages/gravitational_assign.py`, and `src/agent_stages.py::CuratorTopicDiscoveryStage` are the inline references back into this trail.
